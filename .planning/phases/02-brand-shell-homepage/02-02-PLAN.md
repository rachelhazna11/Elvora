## Phase Goal

**As a** visitor, **I want to** land on a complete premium editorial homepage and navigate to brand content pages, **so that** I experience the quiet luxury identity and am drawn into the catalog.

<objective>
Build the five static sections of the homepage: hero (F-001), marquee strip, featured collections (F-002), lifestyle lookbook grid (F-004 / F-044), and brand story (F-005). These sections require no Supabase data — they are pure HTML/CSS with Alpine.js only for the nav behavior already injected by components.js.

Purpose: A visitor can land on index.html and immediately experience the full editorial hero moment and scroll through brand content sections. This is the primary first impression of the Elvora brand.

Output: index.html filled with 5 of the 8 homepage sections. The remaining 3 sections (best sellers, testimonials, newsletter) are delivered in Plan 04 which depends on this plan.
</objective>
